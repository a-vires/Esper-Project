  • What kinds of people does the IT professional interact with? Are they other IT professionals?Clients? Investors? The general public?


    • A. Dan works in an agile team environment, and only reports to the PM (Project manager) and sometimes Directors when he doesn’t like his PM, Its very rare that he will report to other analyst and clients as he tends to avoid confrontation, and prefers to be left alone to code. When other developers ask him for assistance, he sends them the API Documentation rather than pair programming with them, since his is a bit introverted.

  • Where does the IT professional spend most of their time?


    • A. Dan spends most of his time within the offices “dev area” where all the programmers / developer’s hangout. He likes it because the aircon blasted at 100% compacity.

  • What aspect of their position is most challenging?


    • A. The most challenging aspect of Dan’s job is that his able to troubleshoot issues and debug code, since he loves problem solving, he says he can do it all day for the rest of his life because he claims to be a logical thinker.
