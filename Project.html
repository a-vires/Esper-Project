What is the Esper Project?

Esper is a network operated by Blockchain technology to share and utlilise electric and autonomous vehicles. Owners and shareholders of these vehicles recieve from every successful rideshare completion and end users accept terms and conditions when first entering the vehicle. In Australia, we are behind the times in terms of electric vehicle usage and even though governments are trying the impliment more changes this new Esper network gives everyone involved a reason to invest into EVs. With the constant evolution of technology and Australia's constant slow approach to accepting new things its time to realise that the future of transportation is here.
